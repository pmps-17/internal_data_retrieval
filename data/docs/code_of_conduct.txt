# Acme Corp Code of Conduct

1. **Respect & Inclusion**
   - Treat everyone with dignity regardless of role or background.
   - Embrace diversity of thought and culture.

2. **Integrity & Ethics**
   - Always act honestly and transparently.
   - Avoid conflicts of interest; disclose them immediately.

3. **Professionalism**
   - Communicate responsibly, both in person and online.
   - Respect confidential information and intellectual property.

4. **Accountability**
   - Own your mistakes; learn and improve.
   - Give and accept constructive feedback.

5. **Compliance**
   - Follow all local, state, and federal laws.
   - Adhere to company policies and procedures.
