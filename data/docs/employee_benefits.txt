**Employee Benefits Overview**

- **Health Insurance**
  - Medical, dental, and vision plans available.
  - Company covers 80% of employee premium; dependents at 50%.

- **Retirement Savings**
  - 401(k) plan with up to 5% employer match.
  - Vesting schedule: 25% per year, fully vested at 4 years.

- **Wellness Programs**
  - Annual gym reimbursement up to $300.
  - Access to virtual mental health counseling.

- **Professional Development**
  - $1,500 yearly training stipend.
  - Internal mentorship and lunch-and-learn sessions.
