**Acme Corp Vacation Policy**

- **Eligibility:** All full-time employees accrue 1.25 days of PTO per month.
- **Carryover:** You may carry over up to 5 days into the next calendar year.
- **Blackout Dates:** PTO requests are not approved during end-of-quarter reporting weeks.
- **Request Process:**
  1. Submit a “Time Off” request in Workday at least 2 weeks in advance.
  2. Manager approval required.
  3. HR will confirm and update your leave balance.
