# Data Privacy & Protection Policy

1. **Data Collection**
   - We collect only the data necessary to provide services.
   - Personal data is stored encrypted at rest.

2. **Access Controls**
   - Role-based access for all internal systems.
   - Quarterly audits of user permissions.

3. **Data Sharing**
   - No sharing with third parties without explicit consent.
   - Anonymized data sets permitted for analytics.

4. **Retention & Deletion**
   - Retain user data for up to 5 years.
   - Upon request, we delete personal data within 30 days.

5. **Incident Response**
   - Report breaches to CISO within 1 hour.
   - Notify affected users within 72 hours per GDPR.
